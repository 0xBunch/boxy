- **Find connections:** Ask `"Does this relate to any Ideas I'm already working on?"`
- **Seed a new Idea:** Say `"This could be an Idea-draft one for me"` and Boxy will propose a title, summary, and lens connections
- **Quick link:** Say `"Link this to \\\[Lens Name\\\]"` to add the connection without leaving the page
